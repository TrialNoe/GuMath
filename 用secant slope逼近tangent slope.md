> [!example] 命题
> find (a) the slope of the curve at the given point P, and (b) an equation of the tangent line at P. $$y=x^{2}-5,\ P(2,1)$$


figure equation: 
$$

\begin{align}
slope \ equation & =\frac{(2+x)^{2}-1}{x}\\
& =x+ 4
\end{align}

$$
if x approach to 4,then we have :

$$
\begin{align}
 tangent\ slope\ &= 4\\
tangent\ equation &=1+4(x-1)\\
&=4x-3
\end{align}
$$
